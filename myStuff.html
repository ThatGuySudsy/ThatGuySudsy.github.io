The Symbols of Babel

A Python program that creates different black and white 16x16 "symbols". You can see a random one, enter an id, or go in a sequence of every symbol. There are over 1*1077 unique combinations. Everything is displayed in the terminal.

I haven't yet finished another console game, but improved at making them since making this, and will probably finsih one of them up soon.
